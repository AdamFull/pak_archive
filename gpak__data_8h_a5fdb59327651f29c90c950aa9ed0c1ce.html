enum gpak_compression_lz4
Brief Description:\n Enumeration representing the compression levels for LZ4 algorithm in G-PAK.

This enumeration contains values representing the different compression levels that can be used with the LZ4 compression algorithm in G-PAK. The higher the compression level, the better the compression ratio but the slower the compression speed.

Enumerator
GPAK_COMPRESSION_LZ4_NONE 

No compression is applied.

GPAK_COMPRESSION_LZ4_FAST 

Fast compression with lower compression ratio.

GPAK_COMPRESSION_LZ4_MEDIUM 

Medium compression level, balancing compression speed and ratio.

GPAK_COMPRESSION_LZ4_BEST 

Best compression level with higher compression ratio but slower compression speed.
